Sales & Profit Optimization Analysis for a Retail Business                                                                                                                                                                                 
Project Overview                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
This project analyzes the Superstore dataset to identify sales trends, profit drivers, and opportunities for business improvement. The project demonstrates the complete data analysis process, from data cleaning to visualization and business recommendations.                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
Retail businesses often struggle to understand which products, regions, or 
customer segments are driving profit versus loss. High sales do not always 
translate into high profitability, especially when discounts, costs, and 
regional performance vary. The lack of clear visibility into these factors 
makes it difficult for decision-makers to optimize pricing strategies, product 
offerings, and resource allocation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
Objectives
- Clean and prepare the dataset
- Explore sales and profit trends
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Create interactive dashboards
- Present findings using a professional datafolio
